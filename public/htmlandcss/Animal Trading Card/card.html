Red Lionfish!

Fish are gill-bearing aquatic craniate animals that lack limbs with digits

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • ClassActinopterygii
  • Order: Scorpaeniformes

The red lionfish (Pterois volitans) is a venomous coral reef fish in the family Scorpaenidae, order Scorpaeniformes. P. volitans is native to the Indo-Pacific region, but has become an invasive species in the Caribbean Sea, as well as along the East Coast of the United States and East Mediterranean.
